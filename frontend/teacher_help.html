How to use


Q1. How to logout?

Ans: Click on the logout button at the left bottom of the navigation bar.


Q2. How to edit my profile details?

Ans: Click on the settings option from the left navigation bar. After filling the required columns, click on update.


Q3. How to edit, delete, or add new student records?

Ans: Click on the records option from the left navigation bar. The list of all the students will be displayed there. Use the edit or delete button provided along with every student detail column. To add a new student, use the box provided on the right.


Q4. How to view the results?

Ans: Go to the results option from the left navigation bar to view the results.


Q5. How to conduct exams?

Ans: Navigate to the exams tab by clicking on the exams button from the left navigation bar. New tests can be added, and old ones can also be deleted according to your preference. After adding a test, click on the edit icon to add questions.


Important notice: Once the test questions have been added, do not try to update the questions. It may cause errors in the website's functioning. Updating existing questions will be introduced in future updates.
